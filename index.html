Message From Our Brothers

Welcome to the Nu Gamma Chapter of Theta Tau! We are a Professional Engineering Fraternity at Binghamton University. Our fraternity is the oldest and largest engineering fraternity in the United States. We are the first professional engineering fraternity on campus with 150+ in active brothers and alumni. We dedicate ourselves to develop and maintain a high standard of professional interest and to unite us in a strong bond of fraternal fellowship. We welcome all students studying in engineering field to join us at our events.


Adam "Echo" Weiner
