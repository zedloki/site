Professional Development

Theta Tau is first and foremost a professional organization that prepares its members to be the future CEOs and inventors of the engineering field. Throughout the year, brothers hold meetings on campus based on professional values that could be taught or improved on. These workshops are organized by a committee that researches relevant information in order to get ahead. In the past, such events have spoken on creating better linkedin profiles, filing for patents, and proper form of attire. Our brothers are constantly on the lookout for the newest tips to pick up on in order to give every member that inside advantage to succeed in their goals.

Aside from professional knowledge, the fraternity also offers assistance in academic matters. There are older brothers across every engineering field offered at binghamton. We offer assitance to any younger brother who may be struggling in a course in order to set them back n the right track. We try our best to not only make sure that we prepare ourselves, but our brothers around us are ready to tackle anything ahead of them as well.
